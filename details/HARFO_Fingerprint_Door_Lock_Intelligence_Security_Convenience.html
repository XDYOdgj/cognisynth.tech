Introducing the HARFO Fingerprint Door Lock

Elevate your home security with our smart and durable fingerprint door lock. Designed for modern living, it brings advanced features like keyless entry, app control, and voice integration to your everyday routine. Whether you're relaxing at home or away, control your door from your smartphone using Alexa or Google Assistant with ease.

This lock is built to last, with a slim design that fits 35mm-55mm thick doors and a reversible handle that offers left and right-handed access. For added reassurance, it comes with optional backup key and a 12-month warranty, ensuring your safety is always protected.
