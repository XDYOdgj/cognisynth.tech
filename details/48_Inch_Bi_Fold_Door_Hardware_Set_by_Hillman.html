Hillman Bi-Fold Door Hardware Set

Transform your space with the Hillman Bi-Fold Door Hardware Set, designed for a seamless and secure installation. This comprehensive kit includes all the essential components for a professional finish, making it the perfect choice for those looking to upgrade their entryway or sliding doors.

Key Features

  • 48-inch track for a perfect fit
  • 6 non-mortise hinges and 2 of each pivot and roller guide
  • 2 jamb bracket supports and 2 wood knobs
  • Door bumper and aligners included
  • Brass finish for timeless elegance

Why Choose This Set?

Whether you're installing a large sliding door or a bi-fold door for a modern interior, this hardware set is built to deliver durability, precision, and style. The 48-inch track ensures smooth operation, while the non-mortise hinges and pivots provide a strong yet easy-to-install structure. The included jamb bracket supports and door bumper make the process simple and efficient, helping you achieve a polished look without extra effort. With its blade finish, it adds a touch of sophistication to any design.

Ideal for homeowners and designers seeking a high-quality, functional, and aesthetically pleasing solution for their bi-fold doors. Available in the US from China.
