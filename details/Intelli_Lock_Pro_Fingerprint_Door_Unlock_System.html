ENOKER Intelli-Lock Pro - The Ultimate Smart Door Lock

Upgrade your home security with ENOKER's Intelli-Lock Pro, a smart, modern door lock that offers multiple unlocking methods in one sleek design. Whether you're using fingerprint scans, passcodes, or your TUYA account, this lock makes it easy to gain access, with no need for physical keys. It's the perfect blend of convenience and protection for your door.

Why Choose ENOKER Intelli-Lock Pro?

  • Multiple Unlock Options: Fingerprint, passcode, TUYA app, 2x IC cards, and 2x keys. Choose the method that suits your lifestyle.
  • Smart Integration: TUYA Bluetooth Controller (no WIFI, no fees) allows control and monitoring from anywhere, anytime.
  • Fingerprint Recognition: With a response time of 0.2 seconds and 99% accuracy, this large area sensor ensures quick and reliable unlocking.
  • Auto-Lock & Anti-Lock Function: Secure your door automatically and ensure only authorized users can access it with the anti-lock feature.
  • Long Battery Life: Enjoy up to 12 months of usage with built-in emergency power backup and 50 unlock attempts on a single charge.

Designed for Everyday Use

The Intelli-Lock Pro is easy to install and fits most US doors without drilling. Walk in confidently, knowing your entrance is secure and your keys are safely stored away. Backed by a 1-year warranty, this is a trusted product for smart living and business environments.

Peace of Mind, Always

With the added benefit of an optional backup battery and USB power bank, this smart door lock is ready for any situation. Whether you're at home, at work, or on the go, ENOKER's Intelli-Lock Pro ensures your safety, convenience, and control are always within reach.
